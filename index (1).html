Common Questions

The things you are probably thinking right now.

We have heard every objection. Here are honest answers to the ones that come up most.

Social media does not work for cleaning companies or barbershops.
+
People Google your business name after seeing you on Instagram. A cleaning company with 200 followers and consistent posting gets more calls than one with 2,000 followers and no posting history. The algorithm is not the product. Consistency is the product. When a homeowner is ready to hire a cleaner they look up the business they have been seeing every week. When someone wants a fresh cut they book the shop that has been on their feed. Virality is not the goal. Presence is.
I do not have time to film videos or be on camera.
+
You do not need to. Our content does not require you to be on camera. We use professional photos matched to your brand, written posts that sound like you wrote them, and branded graphics that look like a design agency made them. You spend five minutes reviewing each week. We handle everything else. When you are ready to add video we can guide you through a simple 30-second phone format that requires zero editing on your end.
It is too expensive. I cannot justify this right now.
+
One new recurring cleaning client is worth $200 to $400 per month for as long as they stay. One new regular at your barbershop is worth $50 to $100 per month for years. Our service starts at $397 per month. If consistent social media brings you one new client in your first month, the service has paid for itself. The question is not whether you can afford it. The question is whether you can afford the empty weeks.
What if I do not like the content?
+
We revise until you love it. Every single post. If something does not sound like you, flag it and we rewrite it at no extra charge. Most clients need one or two revision rounds in the first month and then our system has your voice dialed in. After that revisions become rare. We also start with a free sample pack before you pay anything — so you see exactly what you are getting before you commit.
What if I want to cancel?
+
Cancel anytime. No contracts. No cancellation fees. No awkward conversations. We earn your business every single month or you leave. Simple as that. We are not interested in clients who stay because they forgot to cancel. We want clients who stay because the content is working.
How do I know this will actually get me more clients?
+
We cannot guarantee new clients because we do not control whether someone calls you after seeing your post. What we can guarantee is that you will be showing up consistently in front of local people who need exactly what you offer — which is more than most of your competitors are doing. Consistency compounds. Month one you are getting noticed. Month three you are the familiar option. Month six people are choosing you before they even compare prices.
