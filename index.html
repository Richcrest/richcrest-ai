FAQ

Questions we get all the time

How does the content actually sound like my business? +
Your intake brief tells us your voice, your ideal customer, what makes you different, and even a post you already love. We use all of that so every caption sounds like you wrote it, not like it came from a template.
When do I get my content? +
Your first content pack is delivered within 24 hours of submitting your brief. After that, content is delivered on a weekly schedule so you always have posts ready to go.
What platforms do you create content for? +
Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X (Twitter), Google Business, and LinkedIn. Every post is written specifically for that platform, not the same caption copied to all of them.
Do I need to know anything about social media? +
Not at all. You fill out a brief about your business and we handle everything else. You copy, paste, and post. Or connect a scheduler and it posts automatically.
Is there a contract? +
No contracts. Month to month only. Cancel anytime with no penalties or fees. We keep clients by doing great work, not by locking them in.
What if I don't like a post? +
Just tell us and we'll rewrite it. Your content should feel like you. If something doesn't fit your voice or your business we fix it, no questions asked.
