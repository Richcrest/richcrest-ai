Common Questions

The things you are probably thinking right now.

We have heard every objection. Here are honest answers to the ones that come up most.

AI video looks fake or cheap. Won't mine too?
+
That happens when someone writes a prompt straight into an AI tool with no direction behind it. We do not do that. Every campaign starts with a creative brief: emotion, story framework, camera language, lighting, and motion, all decided before anything is generated. The AI executes a director's decision, not a guess. That is the difference between a commercial and a novelty clip.
I don't have footage, actors, or a film crew.
+
You do not need any of that. No filming required on your end. We produce the Hero Commercial and UGC review from your product details, brand direction, and a creative brief. If you do have real product photos or footage, we can incorporate them, but it is not a requirement to get a finished campaign.
It's too expensive. I cannot justify this right now.
+
A single entry-level commercial from a traditional studio runs $2,500 to $5,000, and a UGC review video alone averages $500 to $1,200 on top of that. Our Single Campaign starts at $397 and includes both, plus six more deliverables, shipped in days instead of weeks. The question isn't whether you can afford a campaign. It's whether you can afford to keep launching without one.
What if I don't like the campaign?
+
We revise until it's right. Every campaign. If the direction doesn't match your brand or the tone feels off, flag it and we rework it. We also start with a free campaign concept before you pay anything, so you see the direction before you commit to production.
Can I just get social posts instead of video?
+
Yes. Video and full campaigns are our primary focus, but our original weekly social content system, captions, branded graphics, story sequences, is still available as a standalone add-on starting at $397/mo, or bundled free into the Full Studio plan. Think of it as the cherry on top of the campaign work, not a replacement for it.
How do I know this will actually convert?
+
We cannot guarantee sales because we don't control the checkout page or the offer itself. What we can guarantee is that the campaign is directed to build the specific emotion your category needs, premium feel for product brands, craving for food, transformation for fitness, trust for UGC reviews. That is the leverage a real commercial gives you that a phone photo cannot.
